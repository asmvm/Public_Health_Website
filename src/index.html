About Us

Our Mission

INSIGHT empowers diverse organizations to develop, enhance, and expand social, educational, and health-related programs and services through evidence-based monitoring and evaluation.

Our Plan

Each evaluation design is custom built for unique programming, but our strategy and approach to evaluation remains consistent in evidence-based practices. We are guided by the CDC’s evaluation framework and the principles of the American Evaluation Association. Our agency strives to provide learning opportunities for our clients to build evaluation capacity

Our Vision

To empower organizations and subsequently improve the health and wellbeing of all communities.

Services

We work with public and private agencies to monitor and evaluate programs related to behavioral health, substance use, suicide prevention, bullying prevention, education, social marketting, and mental health
