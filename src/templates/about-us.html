Our Story

INSIGHT started out as Smith Consulting Group services in 2003. Women owned and operated, this organization has supported nonprofits, state agencies, academic institutions, and other organizations across the state of Georgia.


  • Our Mission
  • Our History
  • Our Team

INSIGHT empowers diverse organizations to develop, enhance, and expand social, educational, and health-related programs and services through evidence-based monitoring and evaluation.

INSIGHT is a solution driven consulting firm empowering organizations with the information, tools, and resources needed to develop, enhance, and expand programs and services throughout their communities.
